Featured Recipe:Buttermilk Waffles

Features of Recipe

Prep Time
10 minutes
Cook Time
5 minutes
Total Time
15 minutes
Servings
approximately 8 waffles
Calories Per Serving
240 calories per waffle

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 and 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs,lightly beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
Instructions
  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In a seperate bowl, whisk together buttermilk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add melted butter in bowl with wet ingredidents. Whisk until well combined
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until combined. Be careful to not overmix.
  5. Turn on waffle iron and spray with non stick cooking spray.
  6. Once the iron is warm (about 5 minutes), scoop waffle batter into iron. I recommend using about 2/3 to 1 cup of batter per waffle.
  7. Allow waffle to cook until golden brown.Remove from iron carefully.
  8. Repeat for remaining batter. Spraying in between each waffle allows for easier flipping.
  9. Serve with desired toppings. Syrup and whipped cream is optional but delicious!
Note

Recipe is a family recipe that was passed down from my mother.
