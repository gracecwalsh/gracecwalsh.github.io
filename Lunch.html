Featured Recipe: Grilled Cheese and Tomato Soup

Features of Recipe

Cook Time
30 minutes
Prep Time
10 minutes
Total Time
40 minutes
Servings
4 servings for soup, 1 serving of grilled cheese
Calories per serving
257 calories for soup, 300 for grilled cheese

Ingredients

For Tomato Soup

  • 6-8 heirloom tomatoes, cut into quarters
  • 1 small sweet onion, cut into quarters
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 cup canned coconut milk (can be any other non-dairy alternative or milk alternative)
  • 1/3 cup basil pesto
  • optional: parmesan for serving

For Grilled Cheese

  • 2 pieces of thicker sliced bread. I recommend sourdough
  • 1 tablespoon of butter. Salted or unsalted based on individual preference
  • Shredded Cheese of choice. I recommend a sharp cheddar alone or combined with pepper jack for a nice spice kick.
Instructions

For Tomato Soup

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F
  2. In a large oven safe pot, add tomatoes, onion, olive oil, thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Roast in oven for 20-30 minutes. Let cool.
  3. Transfer the tomatoes and juices to a blender. Add in the milk and combine until smooth. Return the soup to the pot and place on stove top over medium heat.Stir in pesto.Addtionally milk can be added to thin as necessary.
  4. Serve in bowls and top with parmesan and basil

For Grilled Cheese

  1. Butter the toast. Spread 1/2 tablespoon of butter on each piece of bread
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add 2 slices of bread with the butter side down.
  3. Stack cheese on one piece of bread. Cover with other piece of toast and flip the sandwich over. Cook until both sides are golden brown and cheese is melted.
