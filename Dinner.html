Featured Recipe: Balsamic Chicken

Features of Recipe

Prep Time
15 minutes
Cook Time
20 minutes
Total Time
35 minutes
Servings
4 servings
Calories per Serving
419 servings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup plus 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (divided)
  • 1 Tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon honey (divided)
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 and 1/4 teaspoons salt (divided)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil (divided)
  • 1 small red onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pints cherry or grape tomates, sliced in half
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 4 ounces part skim bite size mozzarella pears
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
Instructions
  1. Combine 1/2 cup of the balsamic vinegar and 1 tablespoon of honey in a small saucepan. Allow this to simmer over medium-low heat, stirring occassionally, until it is thickened.This should take about 15 minutes, but keep an eye on it.
  2. Remove and set aside from heat
  3. Lightly pound the chicken breasts into 1/2 inch thickness. I recommend putting the chicken between plastic wrap. Then season both sides of the chicken with 3/4 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  4. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and let cook until lightly browned (about 4-5 minutes)
  5. Flip chicken and allow the other side to cook for about 3 minutes. Check chicken for doneness (no visible internal pink)
  6. If the chicken needs more time, flip again and allow it to cook for a few more minutes on each side. Cooking times will vary for size and thickness of chicken breasts. Remove to a plate
  7. In the same skillet, reduce the heat to medium and add remaining tablespoon of oil. Once the oil is warm, add red onion and let it cook until it is softened, about 4 minutes.
  8. Add the garlic and let it cook until fragrant, about 1 minute
  9. Add the tomatoes, thyme and remaining 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar and teaspoon of honey. Season with 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of pepper. Cook until the tomatoes begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
  10. Add the chicken back into the skillet and scatter the mozzarella pearls throughout.
  11. Cover the pan and remove from heat. Let it stand for 1 to 2 minutes until the mozzarella becomes melty
  12. Uncover and drizzle the balsamic reduction over the top. Sprinkle with fresh basil and serve warm.
